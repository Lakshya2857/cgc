About CGC

CGC Mohali is one of the premier universities in North India offering quality education in engineering, management, and applied sciences. With modern infrastructure, it provides excellent learning opportunities.

  • Established in 2001
  • NAAC Accredited
  • Strong Placement Records

Courses Offered

  1. B.Tech (CSE, ECE, Mechanical, Civil, AI & DS)
  2. MBA, BBA, BCA, MCA
  3. Ph.D Programs

MBA 2 Years Graduation
Ph.D 3-5 Years Masters
